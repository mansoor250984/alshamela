Admission Process

  • To start the admission procedure please submit the online Application Form or contact us to schedule a family visit with the head of school. This family interview allows us to share the goals, practices and policies of the school. In return, we expect to learn about your family's goals and your child's specific needs. During this visit the application form will be completed.
  • A follow up interview will be scheduled for the child/children solely with the classrooms teacher. A acceptance letter will then be delivered to the parents and or guardians.
  • Upon receipt of the acceptance letter, parents must accept or refuse to join Al Shamelah Montessori School within three business days. If you do not reply accordingly, the offered spot will be deferred to another candidate.
  • Acceptance of placement in the program is confirmed by the submission of a signed enrollement contract and a deposit of 1,000 Saudi Riyals deductible from the Semester Fees. No student is considered enrolled until the deposit is received.
